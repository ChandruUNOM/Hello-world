INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ADVANCES IN CHEMISTRY WITH SPECIFIC REFERENCE TO CATALYSIS, SENSORS, DRUG DELIVERY AND ENERGY MATERIALS
January 9 - 10, 2020

Department of Physical Chemistry, University of Madras, Guindy Campus, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Brochure

International Conference on Physical Chemistry, will be organized around the theme "INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ADVANCES IN CHEMISTRY WITH SPECIFIC REFERENCE TO CATALYSIS, SENSORS, DRUG DELIVERY AND ENERGY MATERIALS" - ICACSEM 2020 is comprised of Plenary & Invited Lectures and speakers sessions on latest cutting edge research designed to offer comprehensive global discussions that address current issues in Physical Chemistry 2020.

This conference will focus on various aspects of Physical Chemistry with specific attention to Catalysis, Nanomaterials, Quantum Dots, Sensors, Energy Conversion and Molecular Modelling involving Nanoscience and Nanotechnology. This event will provide a platform for the interaction amongst the eminent researchers from both academia and industries at international level under one roof to discuss the state of art of development in Physical Chemistry topics of current interest. It aims at exploring topics in diverse fields of Chemistry, Physics, Engineering and Biotechnology. The conference will provide an overview of the current status and enlighten the future potentials of nanomaterials for physical chemistry and related fields.
